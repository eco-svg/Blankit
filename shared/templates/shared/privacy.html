Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Veyra ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. By using Veyra, you agree to this policy.

1. What we collect

  • Account information: Username, email address, password (hashed — never stored in plain text), date of birth.
  • Profile data: Age tier, distro preference, habits, notes, goals, and other content you create.
  • Usage data: Feature interactions, habit logs, streak data, and community posts.
  • Communications: Messages sent via the DM feature (stored to deliver them).
  • Student verification (optional): If you choose to verify, we temporarily store a document image; only extracted metadata is retained after review.

2. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide age-appropriate features and peer matching.
  • To send account-related emails (OTP verification, password reset, notifications).
  • To display contextual, non-behavioural advertising (based on your stated interests, not tracking).
  • To improve the platform and fix bugs.

3. Children's privacy (COPPA / DPDP)

Veyra is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, you may not create an account.

If we discover that a user under 13 has created an account, we will delete their data promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has registered, contact us at privacy@veyra.app.

Users aged 13–17 are considered minors. We apply additional protections including no behavioural advertising and limited data sharing.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Email providers (Gmail / Brevo) — to send transactional emails.
  • Database hosting (Supabase / PostgreSQL) — to store account and usage data.
  • Platform hosting (Hugging Face Spaces) — to run the application.
  • Object storage (Backblaze B2) — to store uploaded files.

All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Data retention

  • Active account data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymous usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your rights

Depending on your country, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Object to certain types of processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@veyra.app.

7. Cookies

We use session cookies strictly to keep you logged in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

8. Security

Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

9. International users

Veyra is operated from India. By using the platform, you consent to your data being processed in India and on servers operated by the third parties listed above (which may be located in other countries). We endeavour to comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR (EU), UK GDPR, COPPA (US), and India's DPDP Act 2023.

Russia / China: We do not specifically target users in Russia or China and do not maintain local servers in those jurisdictions. Users in those countries access the platform at their own discretion.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: privacy@veyra.app
