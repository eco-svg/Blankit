Veyra is for ages 13 and up

We'd love to have you — but you need to be at least 13 years old to create an account on Veyra.

Why the age limit?
Privacy laws in many countries (US, EU, India) require special protections for younger users that we're not yet set up to provide.
Our peer matching and community features are designed for teenagers and adults.
We want to make sure Veyra is a safe and appropriate space for everyone.

Come back when you're 13 — your streak will be waiting.

A guide for younger users is coming soon.
