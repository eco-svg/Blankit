Buying on Veyra

You're free to pay the seller directly — cash, UPI, bank transfer, whatever works for both of you.

But if you pay outside Veyra, we cannot protect you against fake, undelivered, or misrepresented goods. You're on your own.

When you use Eyes — Veyra's in-app currency:

  • Your money is held safely and only released to the seller once you confirm you got what you paid for.
  • Sellers are ranked by real buyers — repeat purchases, confirmed transactions. No fake reviews.
  • If something goes wrong, you can raise a dispute and we'll step in.

Eyes are the only way Veyra can guarantee your transaction is safe.

⚠ Eyes top-up is currently available for users with an Indian bank account only. International payment methods are being added — sorry for the inconvenience.
