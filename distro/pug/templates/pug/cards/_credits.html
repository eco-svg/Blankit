Sell Back ⚠ Why you get back less than you paid

Eyes are bought and sold at different rates — the buy rate (what you paid) is always higher than the sell-back rate (what we pay you).

This gap, called the spread, is intentional. It protects the platform from currency tricks: if someone bought Eyes cheap in one country and tried to sell back in another country's currency for profit, the spread makes that trade a net loss — always.

Example: 1,000 Eyes bought for ₹924 → sold back for ₹756. The ₹168 difference is the spread.
